About this home

Remarks: A BRAND-NEW HOME, located just outside the city of Seneca, SC. And an amazing Opportunity! This 2,288 square foot home has 1,526 sqft of heated living space, 3 large bedrooms, 2 full baths, 2 car garage. You can entertain your guest and family for years to come and enjoy the serenity of nature, with the covered front and back porches! This home is designed with a split bedroom plan, with the Master suite on one side and 2 bedrooms, a bath on the other, and a Large Open Great Room in the middle. And There is NO HOA! The Great City of Seneca has so much for the whole family. Just minutes away from three major lakes Jocassee, Keowee, and Hartwell; and the beautiful nature of the Blueridge Mountains. In additional Clemson University is a very short drive away. This lot is less than an hour from the city of Greenville, and not too far from Atlanta, Georgia, or Charlotte, NC. And you won’t have to go far to find great eats, entertainment, fitness centers, shopping, and PRISMA Hospital. This area is known for the many waterfalls and nature trails, along the Blueridge Mountains! Oconee County is full of Beautiful Nature that you, your family, and friends can enjoy for years to come. This is your opportunity to own a beautiful lot in a wonderful City! A site for your next Brand NEW Home, at an Amazing Price! This builder is able to build this plan or any other plan on this lot or any other lot. Please call for details.
