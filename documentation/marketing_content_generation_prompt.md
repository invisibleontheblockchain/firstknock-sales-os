## 1. Product Context: What is FirstKnock?
FirstKnock is **the field intelligence platform for door-to-door (D2D) sales teams.**
Most D2D teams rely on outdated, spray-and-pray door-knocking methods. FirstKnock completely changes the game by giving them laser-focused targets: **verified, recently sold homes**. 

**How it Works:**
- **3-Step Property Validation Pipeline:** We pull raw property data and run algorithmic heuristics and synchronous API verification (via RentCast and BatchData). We filter out the junk. If a home is on our map, it 100% just changed hands.
- **Optimized Route Engine:** Managers draw a territory on the map. We instantly generate an optimized walking route highlighting only the highest-converting doors (recently sold, high-value homes). 
- **Mobile-First Knocking App:** Field reps get an app optimized for one-handed use, providing GPS proof-of-visit, offline capabilities, historical data on every door, and quick-action buttons (Not Interested, Sold, Callback).
- **Command Center & Team Dispatch:** Managers get an Uber-style dispatch screen. FirstKnock ranks reps based on performance, distance, and availability, and assigns the best routes to the best closers.

**What Problem We Fix:**
- Wasted time knocking on empty rentals, non-decision makers, or broke homeowners. 
- High rep churn because knocking cold doors is brutal. We give them "warm" lists of new homeowners who are actively spending money on their new houses.
- Lack of accountability and analytics for team managers (we fix this with GPS proof, conversion heatmaps, and real-time team stats).
