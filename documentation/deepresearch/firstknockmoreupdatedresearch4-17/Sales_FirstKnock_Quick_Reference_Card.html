FirstKnock Founder Quick Reference Card

MINDSET RESET: "I built this. I believe in this. I'm calling to find people who have the problem I solved."
Qualifier Flow
Got the name → "Hey [Name], did I catch you at a bad time?"
No name → "Who runs the sales team over there?"
Gatekeeper → "[Company] is hiring reps — I built something for growing D2D teams. 30 seconds with [Owner]?"
Voicemail (Pest) → "Hey [Name], [YOUR NAME] — your reps are knocking 35-50% rentals right now. Built something that fixes that. [PHONE]. Takes 30 seconds."
Voicemail (Security) → "Hey [Name], [YOUR NAME] — new homeowners are your #1 buyer and your reps have no way to find them systematically. Built the fix. [PHONE]."
Pest Control Pitch
  • 35-50% of doors = rentals. Can never buy.
  • New homeowners = highest intent in first 90 days
  • Bad doors → rejection → rep churn ($75K-$145K/head)
  • FirstKnock: verified new homeowner map only
  • "What would 8x closes on the same doors mean for your team?"
Security Pitch
  • New homeowners = #1 security buyer segment
  • Window closes fast — 90 days post-move
  • 100 cold doors → 1 close. 40 FK doors → 8 closes.
  • Reps have no systematic way to find these doors
  • "How many new homeowners did your reps hit last week?"
Buying Signals (They're Ready — Go to Close)
  1. "How does it work exactly?"
  2. "What does it cost?"
  3. "How many companies use this?"
  4. "Does it work in [city]?"
  5. "How long to set up?"
  6. "My reps complain about bad leads"
Trial Close
"I don't want to sell you anything today. Let me show you your territory on the map — 15 minutes. If it doesn't blow you away, I'll leave you alone. You free [day] or [day]?"
Calendar Lock
"Perfect. What's your email? Sending the invite right now — you'll see it in 30 seconds." [Stay on until confirmed.]
Top 5 Objections
  • 1 Already have something → "What's your close rate on new homeowners specifically?"
  • 2 Not interested → "Fair — what's working for lead gen right now?"
  • 3 Send me an email → "I will — it's visual though. 15 min screen share does more. Thursday or Friday?"
  • 4 How much? → "What's a new customer worth annually? The math changes fast."
  • 5 Never heard of you → "I built this [X months] ago. That's why I'm calling personally — not sending a rep."
